operational cost. Latest technologies like AI, AR, VR being used in different areas in the broadcast domain
especially with the Online platform exploding at an exponential rate. Most telcos, broadcasters,content
producers, content aggregators are venturing into OTT/IPTV platforms which can give their customers
wholesome experience with the 4G / 5G LTE technology setting in.
